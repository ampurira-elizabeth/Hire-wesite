Better hours leads
to better work

One of the major advantages that Africa gives you over
other countries is the time proximity. For example, the time
difference between New York and Ghana is only 4 hours.
This is much better than the 12 hour difference to countries
like the Philippines. This leads to a better work-life balance
for you and your new employee.
Here is how this shortened time barrier can help your
business:

  • More time spent working 1 on 1
  • More awake and alert employees
  • Better communication between US and Africa
  • Easier training and onboarding
